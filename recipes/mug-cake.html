Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

This single-serve chocolate mug cake is rich, fudgy, and ready in just about 5 minutes from start to finish. No oven required—just a microwave, a mug, and a few pantry staples!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth and no dry spots remain.
  3. Stir in chocolate chips or drop in a spoonful of Nutella if desired.
  4. Microwave on high for 1 minute to 1 minute 20 seconds. The top should look set but slightly gooey in the center.
  5. Let cool for 1-2 minutes before digging in—top with whipped cream, ice cream, or just enjoy as-is!
